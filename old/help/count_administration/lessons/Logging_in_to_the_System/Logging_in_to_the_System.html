Logging in to the System

In order to control access to the Administration functions of the system you are required to log in. Below is a guide to logging in to the system.

Sign in to the System

Go to the web site at http://app.bikeandwalk.org and click one of the "Sign in" links.

Enter your Email Address and Password

On the login form enter your email address and your password. If you have a "user name" set up on the system, you can use that in place of the email address. Your choice.

If everything is alright, the Admin menu page is displayed.

What Went Wrong?

If you get message that says your user name or pasword are invalid, your account may not set up correctly or it was not set up at all.

If you get a message that says you don't have access to the Admin Sit, it means your login was valid but your permissions were not set high enough.

Click the contact us link and we'll try to resolve the issue. Be sure to provide the email address you with to use during sign in.
