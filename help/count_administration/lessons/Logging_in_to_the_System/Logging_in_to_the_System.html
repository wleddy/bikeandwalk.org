Logging in to the System

In order to control access to the Administration functions of the system you are required to log in. We use the "Persona" authentication system which is operated by the non-profit Mozilla Project, the folks behind the FireFox web browser. Below is a guide to signing up with Persona and then logging in to the system.

Sign in to the System

Go to the web site at http://app.bikeandwalk.org and click one of the "Sign in" links.

Get Your Persona Account

If you don't already have a Persona account (or haven't used it in a while) you'll be asked to provide your email address. It needs to be a real email address because they will send a confirmation email for new accounts. After you signup you'll never hear from them again, so don't worry about getting on some mailing list.

Choose a password

Provide your Persona password. This does not (and probably shouldn't ) have to be the same as your email password. It can be anything you like.

After you click "Done", Persona will send an email to the address you specified. Click the link there to be returned to our sign in page.

Click the "Sign in" link again and then enter your password in the Persona page that opens. You will be logged in to the System at that point and the Administration menu will be displayed.

What Went Wrong?

If you get message that says your email address is not on file or that you don't have access to the site, your account was not set up correctly or it was not set up at all.

Click the contact us link and we'll try to resolve the issue. Be sure to provide the email address you used to create your Persona account.
