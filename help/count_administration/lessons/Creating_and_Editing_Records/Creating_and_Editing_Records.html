Creating and Editing Records

As a Count Administrator you will be responsible for creating and maintaining the system records that pertain to your organization only.

For the most part, creating and editing records should be pretty self explanatory. This guide will mostly cover the details that may not be so obvious.

Who's Records?

The system allows for multiple organizations to conduct traffic counts with all the results stored in one place for all to view and analyze. However each organization controls what types of traffic they count and when. You will be maintaining the settings for your organization only.

Any changes you make do not effect other organizations that use the system.

Creating Records

All record list pages have a link labeled "Add a new <record type>" . Just click that to create a new record.

Editing Records

To Edit a record, click one of the links. The editing form for that record will open.

Deleting Records

To delete a record you no longer want, click the "Delete..." link. You will not be able to delete some records, usually in the case where there are Trips already recorded. In that case the "Delete" link will simply be missing.

NOTE: When you delete a record it is GONE! There is no undo. It may be impossible to recover the data. So just be sure.

What Else?

The remainder of this guide will point out some things about certain types of records that may not be obvious.
